Introduction

Greetings from the Great White North! My name is Kyle Wilson and I am the Owner/Web Developer for Bootstrap Roadmap. My better half in this delightful picture is my beautiful girlfriend, Ashley. As Chief Cheerleader, Ashley holds the most important and highly sought after position. We reside in Bellingham, Washington, which is twenty miles from the Canadian Border. Bootstrap Roadmap is a dream that has been a long time in the making. In early 2013, with the support of my family, I decided to go back to school and finish my bachelor’s degree in Web Design and Development from Full Sail University.

All I have ever wanted in life is the ability to go to work and absolutely love what I am doing. The idea of looking forward to Monday could possibly be the best gift for one’s self and that is what Bootstrap Roadmap and our customers mean to us. My designs are inspired by the most recent in User Experience and User Interaction techniques and disciplines. The results of this are websites that will give our customers the most modern and professional appearance in their highly competitive online market. I specialize in front-end developing as well as some PHP backend processes. Have questions? Ask!
